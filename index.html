UX and UI

What is UX?

"UX" stands for "user experience." In web design, UX design is designing and building products or websites that are easy and enjoyable for people to use. It is a very important part of web design, because no one is going to want to use your website or product if it isn't easy to use.

What is UI?

"UI" stands for "user interface." The user interface is the point of contact between humans and computers. It's what humans use in order to interact with computers and get them to do what they want them to do. Good UI design is super important in web dev because if your UI is ugly or not intuitive, users may be discouraged from using your products or websites.
